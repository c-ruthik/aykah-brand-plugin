**Last updated:** 2026-04-30

## Identity

- **Brand name:** Aykah (always capitalized; never "aykah" or "AYKAH" in body copy)
- **Domain:** aykah.ca
- **Category:** Furniture retailer
- **Co-founders:** Sanya Salehani & Salman Saleem
- **Business model:** Canadian-owned, products sourced internationally, sold in Canada only

### Mission

> "Aykah exists to help you write the story of your home, and help organize your life so you can focus on the things that actually matter."

### Tagline & sub-line

- **Tagline:** *Quietly better.*
- **Sub-line:** *Made of what matters.*

### Brand DNA

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Brand values | Comfort · Integrity · Approachability · Trust |
| Personality | Inspiring · Empathetic · Creative |
| #1 differentiator | Customer service & material honesty |
| Reference brands | Quince, Cuyana, Maiden Home, Brooklinen, Parachute, Sundays Furniture |
| Positioning | Article-grade quality + Structube-grade prices + cohesive collection + superior customer service |
| Anti-positioning | We are NOT Structube. Not disposable, not transactional, not cheap. |

### Origin story

Sanya & Salman started as home stagers. When shopping for their own tiny condo, they hit the Canadian wall: beautiful pieces at outrageous prices, or affordable ones with zero service. Aykah was built to fix that gap.

### What NOT to claim

- ❌ "Designed in Canada" — Aykah does not design products in Canada
- ❌ "Made in Canada" — Aykah is a retailer, not a manufacturer
- ❌ "100-night trial" — does not exist
- ❌ "Free shipping" / "Free returns" — see Shipping & Returns below
- ❌ "Lifetime warranty" — warranty is 1 year
- ❌ "Premium" as a brand-wide adjective — banned in copy until the Fall 2026 premium tier launches; after that, only the tier itself can be called Premium

✅ Correct framing: "Canadian-owned furniture, thoughtfully sourced from around the world, sold across Canada."

## Shipping

- **Canada only.** No US, no international shipping.
- Orders **$199 CAD and over:** complimentary delivery (do not say "free")
- Orders **under $199 CAD:** $19.99 CAD shipping fee

---

## Returns

- **Window:** 14 days from delivery
- **Return shipping:** customer pays
- **Restocking fee:** 20% if item is not returned in original packaging
- **Not free.** Never imply free returns.

---

## Warranty

- **Length:** 1 year from purchase
- **Coverage:** original purchaser only (non-transferable)
- **Excludes:** normal wear, misuse, commercial use

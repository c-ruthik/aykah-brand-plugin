Section 03
Voice Rules
How Aykah sounds — everywhere, every time.

Four Core Attributes

1. Natural & Conversational
Like a founder explaining something clearly. Not a marketing department.
2. Confident but Understated
Quality is implied. Confidence comes from specifics, not adjectives.
3. Warm but Not Emotional
Human, not theatrical. Reads well at a kitchen table, not a runway.
4. Design-Aware but Not Pretentious
We know our materials. We don't talk down.

The Voice Spectrum

We AreWe Are NOT
Warm & welcomingStiff or corporate
Confident & knowledgeablePushy or salesy
Premium-feelingPretentious or exclusive
Helpful & educationalCondescending or dumbed-down
Honest & transparentVague or full of marketing fluff
Inspiring & creativeBland or generic

Emphasize vs. Avoid

Emphasize

How furniture fits into everyday life

Function and design working together

Calm, organized, intentional spaces

Subtle details that make a difference

Practical benefits without sounding technical

Avoid

Cliches: "timeless elegance," "elevated living"

Overly emotional or poetic language

Aggressive sales tone

Overly technical or clinical explanations

Comparisons to competitors by name

Section 07
Product Description Template
3-part framework. 200-400 words per product. Voice doesn't change between price points — materials and craft do.

The Framework

Part 1 — The Hook (80-120 words)
How does it feel in a room? Sell the lifestyle. Lead with emotion, follow with one material detail.
Part 2 — The Specs (60-100 words)
Bullet points. Dimensions, materials, features, suspension, certifications. Be exact. Use premium terms.
Part 3 — Care (60-80 words)
Cleaning tips, care frequency, what to avoid. Practical and brief.

Example — $579 Storage Bed (Current Tier)

Aura Lift-Up Storage Bed — Queen · $579
"Solid wood frame. Linen-upholstered headboard. Two underbed storage drawers on soft-close runners. Designed for everyday living, with durability that holds up over time. Quietly better."

Example — $1,899 Storage Bed (Premium Tier, Fall 2026)

Aura King Storage Bed — King · $1,900
"Kiln-dried solid hardwood frame. Belgian linen headboard with hand-tufted detailing. Four soft-close drawers. CertiPUR-US certified foam headboard padding. Precision levelers on each leg. Built to last decades, not seasons. Quietly better."

Note: Voice stays the same. Materials and craft details scale with price. Price reads as a function of what's in the piece.

Do

Warm natural light, golden hour preferred

Styled rooms that feel real and lived-in

Wood + cream + texture palette

Detail shots (grain, stitching, hardware)

Canadian homes (condos, townhouses, real spaces)

8-12 images per product minimum

Don't

Over-staged rooms that feel like a catalogue

Neon accents or trendy colour pops

Low-resolution images

Generic stock photos

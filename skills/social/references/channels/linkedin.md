# LinkedIn channel reference (Aykah social skill)

LinkedIn is fundamentally different from IG/Reels/Pinterest. Different audience (professionals, designers, hospitality buyers, talent, press), different voice (professional + personal, founder-first), different metrics (dwell time, comments, AI-citation longevity), different formats (long-form articles + document carousels). This file is the canonical LinkedIn channel reference for the `/aykah:social` skill.

Last research-anchored: 2026-05-06. Re-verify timing and algorithm signals quarterly.

## Hook patterns (highest-leverage rule on LinkedIn)

LinkedIn truncates posts on mobile at the **"…see more" cutoff**. The hook MUST land before the cutoff or the rest of the post is invisible to most readers.

**Truncation cutoffs (verified 2026):**
- Mobile: ~140 characters
- Desktop: ~210 characters
- Mobile = 57%+ of LinkedIn traffic
- **Safe target = 140 character ceiling for hook visibility**

**Hooks under 10 words outperform longer hooks by 40%.**

### Hook patterns ranked by 2026 engagement

| Pattern | Engagement (avg) | When to use |
|---|---|---|
| Curiosity-gap | 6.8% (top) | Setup for a non-obvious story or insight; ends mid-thought to pull "see more" click |
| Contrarian / "unpopular opinion" | 6.2% | When Aykah has a real counter-take (DTC playbook says no showrooms — we built two) |
| Personal-story | 5.4% | Founder-led posts; generates the most inbound DMs |
| Stat-led | High credibility | When opening with a real Aykah number (years operating, units shipped, etc.) |

### Examples of each pattern (Aykah-specific)

**Curiosity-gap (curiosity → click)**
> "Eight years in, we just did the math on returns. The number surprised us."
> *(98 chars — under 140, leaves payoff for body)*

**Contrarian (counter to industry wisdom)**
> "The DTC playbook says no showrooms. We opened two. Here's what changed."
> *(81 chars)*

**Personal-story (founder POV)**
> "Last week a customer drove 3 hours to sit on a sofa. We sold her a chair instead."
> *(83 chars)*

**Stat-led (specific Aykah number)**
> "12,000 pieces shipped from Burnaby. One thing we got wrong year one: shipping was the hard part, not sourcing."
> *(120 chars — pushing 140 ceiling)*

### Banned hook patterns (cringe / AI-detected / cliché in 2026)

LinkedIn in March 2026 shifted from network-based to **relevance-based distribution**. Generic AI fluff is actively penalized. Avoid:

- ❌ "I just did X. Here's what it taught me about life/business."
- ❌ "Most people get this wrong. Here's why." (followed by generic listicle)
- ❌ "🚀 BIG NEWS 🚀" / emoji walls in headers
- ❌ Single-sentence-per-line broetry with hollow life-lesson kicker
- ❌ "I'm humbled to announce…" / "Thrilled to share…"
- ❌ "Day 47 of building Aykah…" hustle-culture journaling
- ❌ "Game-changer", "disruptor", "10x", "leverage", "synergize"
- ❌ Pasted inspirational quotes
- ❌ "Agree?" / "Thoughts?" engagement-bait endings
- ❌ "🧵 1/12" thread-pretense (LinkedIn isn't Twitter)

LinkedIn now matches post topic to profile job-title/skills metadata. **Off-topic posts get suppressed.** Sanya & Salman should only post on furniture, retail, sourcing, Canadian DTC ops, design taste, returns/logistics, showroom operations. NOT generic leadership lessons.

## Format rules

**Length:**
- **Posts: 1,300–1,900 characters** (sweet spot — median engagement 2.61–2.67%)
- Hard limit: 3,000 characters
- **Articles: 1,500–2,000 words**

**Hashtags:**
- **3 hashtags = safe default**
- 0 hashtags acceptable for founder thought-leadership posts
- 5 max — never more
- Use specific Aykah-relevant tags: `#CanadianDesign`, `#DTCFurniture`, `#PremiumFurniture`, `#InteriorDesignTrade`, `#FurnitureRetail`, `#BuildInPublic`

**Line breaks:**
- 1–2 sentences per block (white space increases reading time → feeds dwell-time signal)
- Don't go full broetry (every line a new paragraph) — that's the AI-detected pattern now
- Aim for natural breath-points, not artificial drama

**Image direction (the skill emits a brief, doesn't produce the asset):**

Format performance ranking (2026):
1. **Document/PDF carousels** — 6.60–7.00% engagement, up to 4× other formats. **Default for Aykah educational/category posts.** 7–10 slides.
2. **Native video** — 5.60–6.47%. Short (<60s) outperforms longer by 53%. Always upload native (YouTube/Vimeo links: 50–70% less reach).
3. **Multi-image (3–4 images)** — moderate
4. **Text-only** — moderate (better than single image)
5. **Single image** — UNDER-performs text-only by 30%. **Avoid single-hero-image posts.**
6. **LinkedIn Live** — 29.6% engagement (rare format, premium tier)

**Links:**
- **External links = 60% reach penalty.**
- "Link in first comment" is now also detected and penalized as of early 2026.
- Default to NO external link in feed posts. If a link is essential, accept the reach hit.
- Articles can hold links inside the article body — those are not penalized.

## Universal output rules (LinkedIn-specific additions to social SKILL.md)

These apply on every LinkedIn post:

1. **No banned words.** "FREE" → "complimentary"; no "luxury experience", "elevated living", "timeless elegance"; no urgency phrases ("hurry", "act fast", "don't miss out"); no discount framing. Voice gate catches them; never write them in the first place.
2. **No emojis.** (Brand-voice rule applies. LinkedIn allows them but Aykah doesn't.)
3. **No exclamation marks.** Periods. Always periods.
4. **No invented facts.** If a claim isn't in `brand-facts.md` (founders, locations, year founded, units shipped, store count, review counts, policies), ask before writing it.
5. **Stay topic-on-profile.** Sanya/Salman post about furniture, design, retail, sourcing, DTC ops, Canadian retail, showroom moments. NOT generic leadership lessons. LinkedIn algorithm will suppress off-topic posts in 2026.
6. **First 140 characters MUST hold the hook.** If it doesn't read as a complete thought / cliffhanger before the cutoff, rewrite.
7. **No external links in feed posts** (60% reach penalty). Drive traffic via profile bio or follow-up DM. Articles can hold links inside.
8. **3 hashtags default, never more than 5.** 0 hashtags acceptable for founder thought-leadership.
9. **Document carousels and short native video are the format winners.** Default to those for high-effort posts. Single-hero-image posts UNDER-perform text-only — avoid.
10. **First 60–90 minutes after publish drive distribution.** Image-direction brief should include "schedule team to engage in first hour."
11. **Round prices.** $1,899 not $1,899.99.
12. **Soft CTA only** (no "Buy now," no "Limited time"). Most LinkedIn posts work better with NO CTA — let the post itself be the value.

## Articles — additional rules

**When to write an article vs a post:**
- Article = deep-dive / case-study / business-model deep-dive / design-philosophy long-form
- Post = single thought, single moment, single insight
- If the idea fits in 1,900 chars, it's a post. If it needs 1,500+ words to land, it's an article.

**Article structure:**
- Title (declarative + specific number, OR contrarian) — examples: *"Why We Stopped Calling Anything 'FREE' on Aykah's Site"*, *"Eight Years In: What 12,000 Pieces Shipped Taught Us About Returns"*
- Executive summary (3-4 lines, top of article — sets the stakes)
- H2 subheads (4–7 of them)
- 1–2 pull-quotes (specific declarative lines that earn a screenshot share)
- Closing section: forward-looking, brand-anchored
- Author byline: SANYA or SALMAN (never company account — founder articles win for AI citations)

**Article performance reality:**
- Reach: ~596 vs ~921–1,198 for feed posts (lower)
- Engagement: ~12 vs ~21–36 (lower)
- BUT: ~75% of LinkedIn citations in AI/LLM responses come from articles
- Articles are public + indexed by Google + live on a high-authority domain
- 1 article/month compounds for SEO + AI-citation flywheel

**Article cadence: 1/month minimum. Weekly = overkill at Aykah's stage.**

## Common mistakes to avoid (LinkedIn-specific)

| Mistake | Fix |
|---|---|
| Hook over 140 chars (lost on mobile) | Cut to under 140. Test on mobile. |
| Single hero image attached | Switch to document carousel OR short native video OR text-only |
| External link in feed post | Remove. Drive via bio or DM. Or move into an article. |
| Posting outside the Tue–Thu 10–11 AM ET window | Schedule for the right window. First-hour engagement compounds. |
| Off-topic post (e.g., founder posts about parenting) | Algorithm suppresses. Stay on furniture/design/retail/DTC. |
| Broetry walls (1 sentence per line, dramatic) | 1–2 sentences per block. Natural breath-points. |
| 5+ hashtags | Cut to 3 (or 0 for thought-leadership). |
| "I'm thrilled to announce" / "Humbled to share" | Cut entirely. Lead with the substance. |
| Founder post in `company` voice | Match voice mode to author. Sanya/Salman = `sanya`/`salman`. |
| Article under 1,000 words | Either expand to 1,500+ OR convert to a feed post (1,300–1,900 chars). Articles need the long-form length to be worth the lower reach. |

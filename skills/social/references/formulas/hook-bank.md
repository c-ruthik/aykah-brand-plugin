# Hook Bank — Aykah-tone openers

Use as starting material when drafting from scratch. Do not paste verbatim across multiple posts — adapt to the specific product and moment.

These are sorted by hook type. Match the hook type to the channel dial (see `channels/<channel>.md`).

## Founder-voice hooks (use sparingly — Sanya / Salman speaking)

- We started this because we couldn't find what we wanted.
- The version we'd buy ourselves.
- Twelve months in the showroom before this one shipped.
- We charge for the wood, not the words.
- The brief was simple: build it the way it should have been built the first time.

---

## What makes a good Aykah hook

- It reads like a thought, not a headline
- It's specific (a moment, a material, a sound) — not generic
- It works without an exclamation mark
- A friend could say it out loud without sounding weird
- It doesn't try to convince you the brand is good — it just shows you something

---

## What makes a bad hook (do not use)

- "Introducing the new..."
- "Discover the elegance of..."
- "Looking for the perfect [X]?"
- "Have you seen our..."
- "We're so excited to..."
- Anything that ends with "!"
- Lists ("3 ways to...", "5 reasons why...")
- Stock-phrase "lifestyle" lines ("Live your best life", "Home of dreams")

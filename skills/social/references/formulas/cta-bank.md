# CTA Bank — soft CTAs only

Aykah is invitational, not transactional. Every social CTA softens. No urgency, no pressure, no shouting.

---

## Default CTAs (rotate, don't reuse the same one in consecutive posts)

### Discovery

- Explore the collection. Link in bio.
- Shop the look at aykah.ca.
- See more at aykah.ca / link in bio.
- Discover the collection.

### Showroom / in-person

- See it in person — Burnaby, Port Coquitlam, Mississauga.
- Visit the showroom.
- Book a showroom visit at aykah.ca.

### Specific product

- Shop the Aura collection at aykah.ca.
- Now in stock at aykah.ca.
- Available in [color/material] at aykah.ca.

### New arrivals

- Now in the collection.
- New this week.
- Just landed at aykah.ca.

### Quiet / one-line

- aykah.ca
- Link in bio.
- More at aykah.ca.

---

## What's BANNED

These are auto-rejected by the voice gate. Never use:

- Buy now / Shop now (urgency-coded)
- Don't miss out
- Limited time / While supplies last / Last chance
- Hurry / Act fast / Grab yours
- Save $X / X% off / Best price guaranteed
- Click here / Tap to shop (transactional)
- Order today / Get yours
- DM us to order (use the website; the website does this better)

## CTAs by channel

| Channel | Default CTA pattern |
|---|---|
| Instagram feed | "Explore the collection. Link in bio." or "Shop the look at aykah.ca." |
| Instagram carousel (last slide) | "Link in bio." (the swipe is the CTA) |
| Instagram Stories | "Tap to shop." or sticker link, no caption needed |
| Reels / TikTok | "Explore the collection." (overlay text on final frame) |
| Pinterest | "Explore the collection at aykah.ca." (in description) |

---

## When to skip the CTA

For these post types, no CTA is fine:

- Founder content (the post itself is the brand moment)
- Showroom moments / event recaps
- Behind-the-scenes / craft process
- Customer-share / repost (credit the original poster instead)

Forcing a CTA where it doesn't belong reads desperate. Trust the audience to find the site.

# The Aykah Caption Formula

Verbatim from Voice Guide v2 (`core/references/docs/voice-and-language-guide-v2.pdf`, Section 8 — Channel Guide). This is the locked structure for every social caption.

```
[Hook — scene or emotion]
->
[Product + one material detail]
->
[Brand positioning]
->
[Soft CTA]
->
[3–5 hashtags]
```

---

## Step-by-step application

### Step 1 — Hook (scene or emotion)

A line that opens with **a moment, a sensory observation, or a small scene**. Not a feature, not a question, not a generic stock phrase.

- A scene: "Sunday morning, second cup."
- An observation: "The way Belgian linen catches afternoon light."
- A sensory detail: "The sound a solid oak drawer makes when it slides shut."

Do NOT open with: "Introducing...", "Discover...", "We're excited to...", "Have you seen...", a question, or a list.

The hook MUST work in isolation if Instagram truncates after 125 characters.

### Step 2 — Product + one material detail

State the product name and **one** specific material or construction fact. Not a feature list. One detail, named precisely.

- "Aura sectional in oat Belgian linen, kiln-dried oak frame."
- "Aires dining chair, FSC-certified white oak, woven seat."
- "Oslo storage bed, 280 GSM performance bouclé, soft-close drawers."

The detail should be **specific enough to be verifiable**. Avoid vague terms like "premium fabric", "high-quality wood", "durable construction" — those fail the voice gate.

### Step 3 — Brand positioning (light touch)

ONE line that connects the product to a brand idea. Use one of:

- The sub-line: *Made of what matters.*
- A pillar hook (from Voice Guide v2):
  - Materials: "Materials over marketing."
  - Craft: "Built to last decades, not seasons."
  - Living: "Made to settle in." / "For the home you actually live in."
  - Pricing: "Beautifully made — within reach."
  - Service: "Real people, when it matters."
  - Voice: "Quietly considered."
  - Campaign: "Touch wood." / "Substance over surface."

Do NOT use the tagline ("Quietly better.") in every post — it loses meaning. Reserve it for cornerstone posts (launches, founder content, About).

### Step 4 — Soft CTA

Invite, don't pressure.

- "Explore the collection. Link in bio."
- "Shop the look at aykah.ca."
- "See it in person — Burnaby, Port Coquitlam, Mississauga."
- "Now in stock."

Do NOT use: "Buy now", "Shop now!", "Get yours", "Don't miss out", "Limited time".

### Step 5 — Hashtags

3–5 tags. See `channels/instagram.md` for the rotating pool. Do not use 12+ hashtag clouds.

## A complete example

**Brief:** Single-image IG post for the Aura sectional in oat linen.

**Walk-through:**

- Hook: *Sunday morning, second cup.*
- Product + detail: *The Aura sectional in oat linen — Belgian weave, kiln-dried oak frame, low and deep enough to settle into.*
- Positioning: *Made of what matters.*
- Soft CTA: *Explore the collection. Link in bio.*
- Hashtags: *#AykahHome #BelgianLinen #ModernFurnitureCanada*

**Final caption:**

> Sunday morning, second cup. The Aura sectional in oat linen — Belgian weave, kiln-dried oak frame, low and deep enough to settle into.
>
> Made of what matters.
>
> Explore the collection. Link in bio.
>
> #AykahHome #BelgianLinen #ModernFurnitureCanada

Reads at: 41 words, 270 characters, first 125 chars stand alone, 3 hashtags. PASSES the Channel Guide for IG feed.

## Adapting the formula by channel

| Channel | Adjustment |
|---|---|
| Instagram feed | Apply the formula as-is. |
| Reels / TikTok | Hook becomes a 3-second visual + on-screen text. The rest of the formula compresses into the caption under the video. See `channels/reels-tiktok.md`. |
| Pinterest | Drop the scene hook — Pinterest is search-driven. Lead with the keyword phrase. See `channels/pinterest.md`. |
| Threads / X / LinkedIn | Not yet supported in this skill version. |

---

## When the formula doesn't apply

The formula is the default. Override in three cases:

1. **Founder content** — when Sanya or Salman is speaking directly. Use first-person, drop the formula, keep the voice attributes.
2. **Customer service / support replies** — see Voice Guide v2's Customer Service section. Different rules entirely.
3. **Announcement bar / showroom signage** — these are micro-copy, not social. Different file in a future version.
